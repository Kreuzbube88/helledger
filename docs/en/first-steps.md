# 🏁 First Steps

## 1. Create Admin Account

On first launch, HELLEDGER shows a setup form. The first registered user automatically becomes admin.

---

## 2. Onboarding Wizard

After your first login, the **setup wizard** launches automatically and guides you through:

- **Household** — Enter a name for your household
- **Accounts** — Add bank accounts with starting balances
- **Categories** — Select default categories or skip

You can skip the wizard at any time and configure everything manually.

---

## 3. Set Up Accounts

Under **Accounts**, add your bank accounts. Assign a **role** to each account:

| Role | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Main Account** | General-purpose account |
| **Fixed Costs Account** | For auto-booked fixed cost transactions |
| **Variable Account** | For variable day-to-day expenses |
| **Savings Account** | Used for savings rate and savings goals |
| **Separate** | Excluded from all calculations |

Roles allow HELLEDGER to correctly split the **Available** balance in the dashboard and month view.

---

## 4. Set Up Categories

Under **Categories**, configure your categories. There are three types:

- **Income** — e.g. salary, side income
- **Fixed Costs** — e.g. rent, insurance, subscriptions
- **Variable Expenses** — e.g. groceries, leisure

Categories can have subcategories and color codes.

---

## 5. Add Fixed Costs

Under **Fixed Costs**, enter your recurring income and expenses. HELLEDGER automatically books these as transactions every month.

Available types:
- **Income** — e.g. monthly salary
- **Expense** — e.g. rent, utilities, streaming
- **Savings Transfer** — automatic transfer to a savings account

Intervals: monthly, quarterly, semi-annual, annual.

---

## 6. Record Transactions

Under **Transactions**, record income, expenses, and transfers. You can also import transactions from **CSV, OFX, or QFX** files.

---

## 7. Stay on Top of Your Finances

- **Dashboard** — monthly KPIs, account balances, expense donut chart
- **Month View** — detailed breakdown by category
- **Year View** — category × month grid with annual totals
- **Forecast** — 12-month projection based on fixed costs
- **Reports** — charts for expenses, trends, and account balance history
